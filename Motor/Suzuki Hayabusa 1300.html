Suzuki Hayabusa Latest Updates

Suzuki Nepal has launched the 2019 Hayabusa for Rs 13.74 lakh, which is a small hike of Rs 17,000 over the outgoing model. The Busa gets two new colour schemes - Metallic Oort Gray and Glass Sparkle Black. It was one of the most powerful and fastest production motorcycles of its time and is still considered amongst the best drag motorcycles. Mechanically, the 2019 Suzuki Hayabusa is powered by the 1,340cc inline-four engine that produces 195PS of power at 9,500rpm and a peak torque of 155Nm at 7,200rpm.
